# General Mutual Fund FAQ

**Minimum SIP:** For most equity and hybrid schemes, the minimum SIP is Rs 100 per month. Debt schemes may have different minimums; check the SID. Source: https://www.hdfcfund.com/help/faqs

**Factsheet and KIM:** The factsheet and Key Information Document (KIM) contain scheme details, investment objective, asset allocation, riskometer, benchmark, expense ratio and exit load. Source: https://www.hdfcfund.com/legal/fact-sheet

**Investor education:** For general investor education on mutual funds, see SEBI Investor Corner. This is not investment advice. Source: https://www.sebi.gov.in/investor-corner.html

**Regulations:** Mutual funds in India are regulated by SEBI. Scheme documents and disclosures are governed by SEBI regulations. Source: https://www.sebi.gov.in/legal/regulations/mutual-funds.html

Source: https://www.hdfcfund.com/help/faqs
