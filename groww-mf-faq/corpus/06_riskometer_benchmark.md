# Riskometer and Benchmark

**Riskometer:** AMFI's riskometer indicates the risk level of a scheme (e.g. Low, Moderate, Moderately High, High). It is displayed in the scheme's KIM and factsheet. Equity schemes are typically "Moderately High" or "High". Source: https://www.amfiindia.com/riskometer

**Benchmark:** Each scheme has a benchmark index to compare performance. The benchmark is disclosed in the factsheet and SID. Do not use it as a guarantee of returns. Source: https://www.amfiindia.com/scheme-information

**Finding riskometer and benchmark:** Check the latest factsheet or Key Information Document (KIM) of the scheme on the AMC website or AMFI. Source: https://groww.in/mutual-funds/amc/groww-mutual-funds
