# Fees, Charges and Exit Load

**Expense ratio (TER):** Total expense ratio is the annual cost charged to the scheme. It is capped by SEBI and disclosed in the scheme factsheet. Direct plan TER is lower than regular plan. Source: https://www.hdfcfund.com/help/charges-and-expenses

**Exit load:** Exit load is a charge, if any, when you redeem units within a certain period. The structure (e.g. 1% if redeemed within 1 year) is scheme-specific and given in the KIM and SID. Source: https://www.hdfcfund.com/help/charges-and-expenses

**No performance claims:** Returns are not guaranteed. For performance data, refer only to the official factsheet and scheme documents. Source: https://www.hdfcfund.com/legal/fact-sheet

Source: https://www.hdfcfund.com/help/charges-and-expenses
