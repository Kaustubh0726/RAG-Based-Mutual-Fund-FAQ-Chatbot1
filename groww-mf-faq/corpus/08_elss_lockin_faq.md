# ELSS Lock-in and FAQ

**ELSS lock-in period:** ELSS (Equity Linked Savings Scheme) has a lock-in of 3 years from the date of investment. Each SIP instalment has its own 3-year lock-in. No withdrawals are permitted during the lock-in. Source: https://www.hdfcfund.com/products/mutual-funds/equity/hdfc-taxsaver-fund

**Tax benefit:** ELSS offers deduction under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act, subject to applicable limits and conditions. Consult the scheme SID and your tax advisor. Source: https://www.amfiindia.com/investor-corner/faqs

**After lock-in:** After 3 years, you can redeem without lock-in restrictions. Exit load, if any, after lock-in is as per the scheme's KIM. Source: https://www.hdfcfund.com/legal/sid-kim

Source: https://www.amfiindia.com/investor-corner/faqs
