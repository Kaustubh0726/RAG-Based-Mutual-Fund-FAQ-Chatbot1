# Groww ELSS Tax Saver Fund – Expense ratio, lock-in, SIP, exit load

**Expense ratio – Groww ELSS Tax Saver Fund:** Direct Plan 0.94% per annum; Regular Plan 2.3% per annum. Source: https://groww.in/mutual-funds/amc/groww-mutual-funds

**Lock-in period – ELSS:** Groww ELSS Tax Saver Fund has a mandatory lock-in of 3 years from the date of allotment. This applies to all ELSS schemes as per regulations. Source: https://assets-netstorage.growwmf.in/compliance_docs/Downloads/SID/SID_Groww%20ELSS%20Tax%20Saver%20Fund.pdf

**Exit load – Groww ELSS Tax Saver Fund:** Exit load is Nil. The 3-year lock-in applies. Source: https://groww.in/mutual-funds/amc/groww-mutual-funds

**Minimum investment – Groww schemes:** Lumpsum investment Rs 500; SIP investment Rs 500 per month; additional purchase Rs 500. Source: https://groww.in/mutual-funds/amc/groww-mutual-funds
