# How to download account statements and capital gains report

**Method 1 – KFintech portal (RTA):** Visit https://mfs.kfintech.com/investor/General/CapitalGainsLossAccountStatement and select 'Groww Mutual Fund' from the AMC dropdown to get capital gains statement. Source: https://mfs.kfintech.com/

**Method 2 – MF Central:** Visit https://www.mfcentral.com/ and log in with your credentials for a consolidated statement. Source: https://www.mfcentral.com/

**Method 3 – Email request:** Email iro@growwmf.in with subject 'Request for Account Statement' to request account statement. Source: https://mfs.kfintech.com/
