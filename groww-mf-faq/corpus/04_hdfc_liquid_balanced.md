# HDFC Liquid Fund & HDFC Balanced Advantage Fund

**HDFC Liquid Fund (Debt):** Minimum investment and SIP (if offered) are as per SID. Expense ratio and exit load are in the KIM and factsheet. Riskometer for liquid funds is typically "Low to Moderate". Source: https://www.hdfcfund.com/products/mutual-funds/debt/hdfc-liquid-fund

**HDFC Balanced Advantage Fund (Hybrid):** Minimum SIP is typically Rs 100. Expense ratio, exit load, benchmark and riskometer are in the scheme's factsheet and KIM. Source: https://www.hdfcfund.com/products/mutual-funds/hybrid/hdfc-balanced-advantage-fund

**General minimum SIP:** For most HDFC MF schemes, the minimum SIP is Rs 100 per month. Confirm from the AMC FAQ or SID. Source: https://www.hdfcfund.com/help/faqs

**Exit load (general):** Exit load varies by scheme and is disclosed in the Key Information Document (KIM). It is usually applicable if units are redeemed within a specified period. Source: https://www.hdfcfund.com/help/charges-and-expenses

Source: https://www.hdfcfund.com/help/faqs
