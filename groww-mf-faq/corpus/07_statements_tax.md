# Statements and Tax Documents

**How to download statement / capital gains statement:** You can download your account statement and capital gains report from your fund account (through the AMC's portal or the distributor platform you use, e.g. Groww). Log in to your account, go to Statements or Tax/Reports section, and download the capital gains statement for the financial year. Source: https://www.hdfcfund.com/help/statements-and-documents

**Groww – statements and tax:** On Groww, go to Help or your mutual fund holdings and look for "Statements" or "Tax documents" to download capital gains statement and other reports. Source: https://groww.in/help/statements-and-tax

**AMFI – statements:** AMFI provides guidance on statement and document availability. Investors can get statements from their AMC or through the platform where they hold units. Source: https://www.amfiindia.com/investor-corner/statements

**Capital gains (educational):** For understanding capital gains tax on mutual funds, refer to SEBI/income tax educational content. This is not tax advice. Source: https://www.sebi.gov.in/investor-corner/capital-gains.html

Source: https://groww.in/help/statements-and-tax
